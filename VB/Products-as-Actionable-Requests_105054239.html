Volo Bible : Products as Actionable Requests

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar on Mar 16, 2020

Actionable Requests

  1. Select Products from the CAM or click Products + Add from the CAM

  2. Assign an appropriate title (e.g. housekeeping, maintenance request)

  3. Assign a subtitle if desired.  If chosen it will display in the grey text box below the title.

  4. Add a brief description (friendly blurb) of what this actionable item entails.

  5. Choose a photo using the method described in this manual.

  6. Choose action from the button label drop-down menu.

    1. Choose Add for

    2. Choose Book for

    3. Choose Request for

  7. Choose Price from the drop-down menu.

    1. Choose -------- to display no payment information.

    2. Choose $ if a price will be associated and displayed.

    3. Choose Free to reflect that there is no cost associated with the item.

    4. Choose Paid to reflect that this service is included (no cost associated with the item).

  8. Assign a price to the product according to the information provided by the hotel.  Note that items in bold cannot be left blank and the default of 0 may be used if the product is Free or Paid.

  9. Choose a Thankyou from the drop-down menu that will display after this product has been ordered from the system. See Adding Thankyou’s                  

  10.  Confirm that the Listed box is checked.

  11. Confirm that the “Can be ordered” box is checked. (Uncheck if the product is just informative and can’t be purchased, reserved, or otherwise requested.  The button label is ignored if this is not checked.

  12. Confirm that the “Ask for guest’s phone” is checked if the hotel would like to call or text the guest with more information.

  13. Pairing a product for actionable items is not applicable.

  14. Promoting a product for actionable items is not applicable.

  15. Advance Display

  16. Choose and sort options. See Adding Options

  17.  Save 

    1. Click Save to exit the page

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new product.
