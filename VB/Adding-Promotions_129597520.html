Volo Bible : Adding Promotions

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar on Mar 24, 2020

Promotions are upsells that are suggested to the guests when a paired product is ordered from a store. An example would be a suggested glass or bottle of white wine when a salmon entree is placed in the in-room dining cart or a suggested manicure if a pedicure is ordered. Once ordered, the promotion will pop up on the tablet and suggest this as a good addition to the product just ordered. This is a revenue-generating function of the CMS.

Promotions

  1. Click Promotions +Add in the CAM.

  2. Enter a Name for the promotion. The title should coincide with the section the promotion will fall under (e.g. In-Room Dining, Spa) and will be used for reference later.

  3. Enter a Title for the Promotion.

  4. Enter text (short blurb) that describes the pairing.

  5. Add a discount or leave at 0.0. A discount would pertain to a percentage off as a further incentive to purchase the product.

  6. Add a Button Label. “Add To Your Order” is a good default.

  7. Add Thank You Message.

    1. Create a Thank You Title.

    2. Create a Thank you Text.

  8. Save.

    1. Click Save to exit the page.

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page.

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new Promotion.

Next Steps

  1. Click on Products in the CAM

  2. Search for the Product that will initiate the pairing by using the search bar at the top right of the screen.

  3. Click on the Product that will initiate the promotion and enter the page to edit.

  4. Choose the Product you would like to pair with the selected Product from the drop-down menu.
