Volo Bible : Adding Products

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar, last modified on Mar 16, 2020

Products are considered items in a store that can be ordered such as a glass of wine or a salon service from a spa. Products can also be actions that can be requested, such as housekeeping, or turndown service. Creating a product will differ slightly depending on its end-use. The process is broken down into types in the pages that follow.
