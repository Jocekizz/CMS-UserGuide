Volo Bible : Adding Staff

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar on Mar 23, 2020

The CMS has the ability to showcase staff members via a Meet The Staff page which includes space for a photo (headshot) and a short bio. The Meet The Staff page can have up to three staff members per section, section cover, or advanced cover. Adding Staff members also creates the ability in the Staff Portal for personalized messages to the guest, as well as an account for staff members to process orders in the Staff Portal.

Personalized Staff Members

  1. Click the Staffs +Add in the CAM.

  2. Enter the Staff Member’s first name.

  3. Enter the Staff Member’s last name. 

  4. Enter the Staff Member’s role.

  5. Enter a descriptive bio for the Staff Member.

  6. Choose or upload a staff image with dimensions of 174 x 174 pixels using the method described in this manual.

  7. Check the “can admin orders” only if the Staff Member will be receiving orders in the staff-interface

  8. Save

    1. Click Save to exit the page

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new navigation

Entries in bold cannot be left blank!


Generic Staff Members or “Hotel” Staff Member

  1. Click the Staffs +Add in the CAM.

  2. Enter the first name as <Hotel Name> (e.g. Hilton).

  3. Enter the last name as “Staff.”

  4. Enter the role as “Staff.”

  5. Enter “Staff” in the bio container.

  6. Choose or upload a photo in the form of a black or colorized hotel brand logo with dimensions of 174 x 174 pixels using the method described in this manual.

  7. Place a checkmark next to “can admin orders.”

  8. Save

    1. Click Save to exit the page.

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page.

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new Staff Member.
