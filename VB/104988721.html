Volo Bible : Products as Base for PDF/Website

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar, last modified on Mar 23, 2020

Products that will be associated with Websites or PDF’s 

  1. Select Products from the CAM or click + Add from the CAM

  2. Assign an appropriate title (the name of a business, website, or title of PDF).

  3. Leave Subtitle blank 

  4. Leave Description blank 

  5. Do not choose a photo or an icon 

  6. Leave button label as is

  7. Leave Price tag as is

  8. Leave price at 0.00 

  9. Leave Thankyou as is.

  10. Confirm that the Listed box is checked

  11. Confirm that the “Can be ordered” box is unchecked

  12. Confirm that the “Ask for guest’s phone is unchecked

  13. Ignore Promotion Section

  14. Choose an Advanced Cover from the drop-down menu if this product will navigate from one. See Advanced Covers

  15. Ignore Option.

  16. Save 

    1. Click Save to exit the page

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new product.


Next steps
