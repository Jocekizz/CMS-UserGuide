Volo Bible : Products in a Store

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar on Mar 16, 2020

Items/Services in Store

  1. Click Products +Add in the CAM

  2. Assign an appropriate title (e.g. Fettuccine, Thai Massage).

  3. Assign a subtitle if desired.  If chosen it will display in the grey text box below the title.

  4. Add a brief description of the item if required. (e.g. Fettuccine in a white clam sauce).

  5. Choose a photo or Icon for the item using the method described in this manual.

    1. Add note here: In-Room Dining will not require a photo, Item Requests will require a photo, Spa-style store requires a photo.

  6. Choose action from the button label drop-down menu.

    1. Choose Add for

    2. Choose Book for

    3. Choose Request for

  7. Choose Price from the drop-down menu

    1. Choose -------- to display no payment information

    2. Choose $ if a price will be associated and displayed

    3. Choose Free to reflect that there is no cost associated with the item

    4. Choose Paid to reflect that item is included (no cost associated with the item).

  8. Assign a price to the product according to the information provided by the hotel.  Note that items in bold cannot be left blank and the default of 0 may be used if the product is Free or Paid.

  9. Choose a Thankyou from the drop-down menu that will display after this product has been ordered from the system. See Adding Thankyou’s

  10. Confirm that the Listed box is checked.  This may be unchecked if the product is out of stock, out of season, etc. and will not be shown in the Store Menu.

  11. Confirm that the “Can be ordered” box is checked or un-check if the item is just informative and can’t be purchased, reserved or otherwise requested.

  12. Confirm that the “Ask for guest’s phone” is checked if the hotel would like to call or text the guest with more information.

  13. Pair - If you would like to pair the product with another product, to be offered as an up-sell upon the order check-out, select the other product to be paired with.

  14. Promotion - If you paired the product, select which type of store (in-room dining, spa, or item request) these products are being used in.

  15. Advanced Display may be ignored for individual products that will serve as items in a store.

  16. Choose and sort options. See Adding Options

  17.  Save 

    1. Click Save to exit the page

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new product.

Next Steps

  1. Open Store or Category of Store in CAM.

  2. Choose Store or Category of Store the product will be in.

  3. Choose the product from the drop-down menu.

  4.  Save 

    1. Click Save to exit the page.

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page.

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new product.
