Volo Bible : Adding a Section

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar, last modified on Mar 12, 2020

Sections will be either the nine Menu Items that are displayed on the left side of the main screen in the application or “headers” of organization for pages, stores, products, and other sections.  Sections are always represented with an icon.

Main Menu Sections

Note: In the Volo! CMS, fields that have bold titles cannot be left blank!

  1. Click either the + Add or the Change button next to Sections in the Content Administration Menu (CAM).

  2. Create the Title for the Section.

  3. Choose a suitable icon from the drop-down menu.

  4. Choose Cover options.

    1. Leave the Cover blank if this section will not navigate to a cover or if a cover has not been created yet. (See Adding Covers)

    2. Click on the appropriate cover name to add a cover.

  5. Choose a Cross Sell from the drop-down menu.   If this section will not be associated with items for purchase, click any Cross Sell.  (See Creating Cross Sells)

  6. Choose a Thankyou from the drop-down menu.   If this section will not be associated with items for purchase, click any Thankyou.  (See Creating Thankyous)

  7. Check the box marked Enable <<other requests>> if the hotel will be DHD+Messaging or Full Interactive and this Section will allow for requests.

  8. Check the box marked “Enable products ordering” if the hotel will be DHD+Messaging or Full Interactive and this Section will allow for product ordering.

  9. Enter a notifications email for DHD+Messaging and Full Interactive Hotels if the property wants to participate in this function.  A notification email will be sent to this address when a product is ordered from this section

  10. Enter a notifications phone for DHD+Messaging and Full Interactive Hotels if the property wants to participate in this function.  A notification text will be sent to this number when a product is ordered from this section.

Navigations

  1. Choose the Navigation from the drop-down menu (See Adding Navigations). There may be up to 9 navigations per section.

  2. Click +Add another navigation to add more than three navigations at

  3. Reorder the Navigations if needed.

    1. Clicking Save and Continue Editing will allow the navigations to be reordered by refreshing the screen.  

    2. Click and hold down the cursor on the :Reorder: to the left of each navigation.  Move the cursor up or down until the navigations are in the appropriate order.

Staff

If there will be a “meet the staff” entry for this section, you must add staff members. There can be up to three staff members per section.

  1. Choose the Staff Member from the drop-down menu. (See Adding Staff Members)

  2. Reorder the Staff if needed:

    1. Clicking Save and Continue Editing will allow the staff members to be reordered by refreshing the screen.  

    2. Click and hold down the cursor on the :Reorder: to the left of each staff name.  Move the cursor up or down until the staff are in the appropriate order.

  3. Save 

    1. Click Save to exit the page

    2. Click Save and Continue editing to make changes to the existing page

    3. Click Save and add another to add a new section.
