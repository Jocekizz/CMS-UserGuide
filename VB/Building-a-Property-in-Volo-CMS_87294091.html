Volo Bible : Building a Property in Volo CMS

Created by Jocelyne Kizziar, last modified on Jun 15, 2020

The following pages deal specifically with the steps needed to populate the Content Management System for a property.

Most of the specific hotel content will be found on the hotel’s website and/or be submitted by the hotel itself. Local information may be provided by the hotel or can be found using city guides of popular attractions, restaurants, tours, etc.

The first step in on-boarding a hotel client is sending a Content Building Form to the agreed-upon hotel contact. This form will contain important contact information, brand specifications, and features the hotel would like to include.

Next, a hotel blueprint will be created from a template in Google Drive named Standard Blueprint that resides in the “New Clients Template” drive folder.

Third, a meeting will be held with the hotel contact to approve the blueprint and collaborate on what other content the hotel would like to include.
